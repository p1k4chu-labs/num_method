Numerical Methods Playground

An interactive collection of web-based tools for visualizing and solving problems in numerical analysis.

Jacobi Method

An iterative algorithm for determining the solutions of a strictly diagonally dominant system of linear equations.

Gauss-Seidel Method

An iterative method used to solve a system of linear equations. It's an improvement over the Jacobi method.

LU Decomposition

A method for solving systems of linear equations by factoring a matrix into a product of a lower and an upper triangular matrix.
